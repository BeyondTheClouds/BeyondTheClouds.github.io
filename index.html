DVMS

DVMS (Distributed Virtual Machine Scheduler) is the first building block that have been implemented by the initiative. Validated by simulations (100K VMs on top of 10K PMs) and in vivo experiments (5K VMs on top of 500 PMs) , it enables to dynamically relocate VMs throughout the infrastructure in order to satistfy VM evolving needs during their executions. DVMS is fully distributed, relying on a P2P and cooperative approach.
