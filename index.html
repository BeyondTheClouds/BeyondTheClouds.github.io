Beyond the Clouds

Beyond the Clouds!

This initiative aims at overcoming the main limitations of the traditional server-centric cloud solutions.

A Research Initiative for a Fully Decentralized IaaS manager

Fully Distributed

We aim at designing a cooperative Cloud OS that will work in a fully distributed way, following a peer to peer architecture.

Fault Tolerant

At large scale, failures become the norm rather than the exception. Fault tolerance mechanisms will be included in our distributed Cloud OS.

Who we are?

Members of this research initiative are researcher from various fields such a System programming, Overlay Network and Cloud Computing.
